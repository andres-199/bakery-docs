# Bakery System - System Definition

## Overview
The Bakery System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline daily business operations in a simple and organized manner. It covers everything from front-counter sales to backend production and inventory management.

## Core Modules

### 1. Point of Sale (POS)
The POS module allows for fast and agile sales processing.
*   **Quick Registration:** Rapidly select products and specify quantities.
*   **Total Calculation:** Automatic calculation of the total sale amount.
*   **Sale Items:** Each sale stores a detailed record of the items sold.
*   **Cash Sessions:** Sales are associated with a specific cash session to ensure control over revenue.

### 2. Payments
Simplified yet flexible payment processing for every transaction.
*   **Payment Methods:** Supports Cash, Wire Transfer, and Credit/Debit Cards.
*   **Split Payments:** Allows for multiple payment methods per sale (e.g., partial cash and partial card).

### 3. Cash Sessions (Session Management)
Ensures accountability for daily intake and outflows.
*   **Opening:** Start the day or shift by recording the initial cash balance.
*   **Automated Tracking:** Automatically logs all sales conducted during the session.
*   **Closing:** Ends the session with a detailed revenue summary.

### 4. Product Management
Centralized control over the items offered to customers.
*   **Catalog Management:** Create and manage the list of available products.
*   **Pricing:** Define and update selling prices.

### 5. Recipes & Production
Links products to their manufacturing process.
*   **Recipes:** Associate products with specific recipes defining required ingredients and quantities.
*   **Production Lots:** Track daily production per product, including quantities and timestamps.
*   **Automated Consumption:** Inventory levels for raw materials are automatically adjusted (deducted) upon production.

### 6. Inventory Control
Maintains visibility over stock levels.
*   **Raw Materials:** Basic tracking of ingredient availability.
*   **Inventory Movements:**
    *   **Entries:** Record stock increases from supplier purchases.
    *   **Exits:** Track consumption from sales or production.
    *   **Manual Adjustments:** Perform simple manual corrections to stock levels.

### 7. Supplier Management
Manages the source of materials.
*   **Supplier Directory:** Maintain a record of ingredient providers.
*   **Purchase Tracking:** Link inventory entries to specific suppliers for traceability.

### 8. Staff & Access Control
Manages system users and their permissions.
*   **Roles:** Defines basic roles such as **Cashier** and **Administrator**.
*   **Access Control:** Simple permission management based on user roles.

### 9. Customer Relations
Tracks interactions with regular buyers.
*   **Customer Records:** Maintain a list of customers to track purchase history and commercial relationships.

## Primary Objective
The main goal of this system is to provide the bakery with daily operational control over:
*   **Sales Processing**
*   **Cash Management**
*   **Production Planning**
*   **Inventory Tracking**

By centralizing these key operational areas, the Bakery System provides a clear and reliable overview of business performance.
